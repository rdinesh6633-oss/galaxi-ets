Inside the Technology

Built Different.
Engineered Better.

Every subsystem engineered from ground up — not assembled from generic parts.

01

DAB DC-DC Architecture

Dual Active Bridge topology — galvanic isolation between battery and inverter. Gold standard in modern power electronics. Higher efficiency, lower heat, better reliability.

02

~94.5% Inverter Efficiency

Traditional inverters run at 85–88%. That 6–9% difference is real money saved every day, compounding over years. More energy reaches your home, less is lost as heat.

03

DSP-Based Control

Digital Signal Processor manages all power conversion with microsecond precision. Faster, more accurate, and more stable than analog control in traditional inverters.

04

Fast Hardware Protection

Hardware-level fault detection for over-current, short circuit, and thermal events. Reacts in microseconds — before any software can respond.

05

LiFePO4 Battery Chemistry

Safest lithium technology available. No thermal runaway risk. 3,000+ cycles at 80% DoD — 8 to 10 years of daily use under Indian conditions.

FAQ

Quick Answers

Can I add battery modules later?
▾
Yes. Modules use blind-mate connectors and can be added anytime without rewiring. Stack the module and the system detects it automatically.
Can it be wall-mounted like a TV?
▾
Yes. Mounts cleanly like an LED TV. Zero floor space used. Premium aesthetic on any wall. Installation takes under an hour.
Can it stand on the floor too?
▾
Yes. Included support bush allows stable floor-standing. No drilling required. Keep it portable and move it as needed.
Does it support solar panels?
▾
Yes. Built-in solar hybrid input. AI decides whether to use solar directly, charge battery, or draw from grid based on current conditions and your mode.
What is AI Mode?
▾
AI Mode uses your usage patterns, weather forecast, power cut history, and location data to automatically optimize every decision — daily, without any manual input.
Is the 2-year IoT plan free?
▾
Yes. Every unit includes 2 years of free premium IoT — real-time monitoring, smart alerts, and AI optimization from day one. No subscription for 2 years.
Is battery included in the price?
▾
UPS unit and LiFePO4 battery modules are sold separately. Choose exactly the capacity you need and add modules over time as your requirements grow.
Can it run an air conditioner?
▾
3kW model can run a 1-ton inverter-type AC for limited hours depending on battery. Non-inverter ACs and multi-ton units are not recommended.
